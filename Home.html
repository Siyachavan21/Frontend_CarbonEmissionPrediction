Carbon Emissions by Industrial Activities

Carbon emissions are the release of greenhouse gases, or their precursors, into the atmosphere over a specific area and time period.
Industrial activities, such as manufacturing, energy production, and transportation, are significant contributors to carbon emissions.
These activities involve the burning of fossil fuels, chemical processes, and other operations that release large amounts of carbon dioxide
and other greenhouse gases into the atmosphere. By analyzing data from these industries, our project aims to forecast future emissions under
various scenarios, helping to inform decisions that can lead to a more sustainable future.
